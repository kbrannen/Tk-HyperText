Supported Escape Sequences

Tk::HyperText supports the rendering of a few standard escape sequences.

< &lt;
> &gt;
& &amp;
" &quot;
' &apos;
  &nbsp;
© &copy;
® &reg;
♥ &hearts;
♦ &diams;
♣ &clubs;
♠ &spades;

In addition, all standard decimal escape codes are also rendered successfully. For example:

¤ &#0164;
¿ &#0191;
